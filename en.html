About Us

Light of Life for Supplications and Remembrances

Light of Life is a website aimed at providing a variety of supplications and remembrances to enhance spirituality and draw closer to God. The website seeks to provide reliable sources of supplications and remembrances, guiding visitors towards the right path in achieving inner peace and spiritual contentment.

The Purpose of the Website

We aim to provide a stimulating and enhancing online environment for engaging with supplications and remembrances, and to offer spiritual resources that help promote mental comfort and stronger connection with God.

  • We believe in the power of supplication and remembrance in achieving inner peace and spiritual connection.
  • We strive to provide diverse and reliable content to meet the needs of visitors in various times and circumstances.
  • We aim to guide visitors towards the right path in continuing remembrance and supplication in their daily lives.

Supplications from the Prophetic Traditions

Some of the supplications made by the Prophet Muhammad (peace be upon him)

Supplication for Seeking Guidance (Istikhara)

O Allah, I seek Your guidance [in making a choice] by virtue of Your knowledge, and I seek ability by virtue of Your power, and I ask You of Your great bounty. You have power, I have none. And You know, I know not. You are the Knower of hidden things. O Allah, if in Your knowledge, this matter (then it should be mentioned by name) is good for me both in this world and in the Hereafter (or: in my religion, my livelihood and my affairs), then ordain it for me, make it easy for me, and bless it for me. And if in Your knowledge it is bad for me and for my religion, my livelihood and my affairs (or: for me both in this world and the next), then turn me away from it, [and turn it away from me], and ordain for me the good wherever it may be and make me pleased with it.

Supplication for Riding (Traveling)

Glory is to Him who has subjected this to us, and we could never have it (by our efforts). Surely, to our Lord we must return.

Supplication for Fear

O Allah, I seek refuge in You from leprosy, insanity, elephantiasis, and evil diseases.

Supplication before Sleeping

O Allah, in Your name, I die and I live.

The Five Pillars of Islam

The Five Pillars of Islam are the fundamental principles of the Islamic faith.

The Declaration of Faith (Shahada)

Declaring that there is no god but Allah, and Muhammad is the Messenger of Allah.

Prayer (Salah)

Performing the five daily prayers.

Charity (Zakat)

Giving a portion of one's wealth to those in need.

Fasting (Sawm)

Observing fasting during the month of Ramadan.

Pilgrimage (Hajj)

Performing the pilgrimage rituals during the designated time.

The Six Pillars of Faith in Islam

The Six Pillars of Faith in Islam are fundamental beliefs that every Muslim must uphold.

Belief in Allah Alone

Believing that there is no god but Allah, and that Allah is the Creator and Sustainer of all things.

Belief in the Angels

Believing in the existence of angels and their role in carrying out the commands of Allah and guarding humans.

Belief in the Heavenly Books

Believing that Allah revealed heavenly books to the prophets and messengers to guide humanity.

Belief in the Prophets and Messengers

Believing that Allah chose messengers and prophets to convey His message to humanity.

Belief in the Last Day

Believing in the resurrection, judgment, and accountability on the Day of Judgment.

Belief in Destiny, Good and Evil

Believing that everything happens by the will and decree of Allah, whether it is good or evil.
